1) Install Customizable Player Models - https://modrinth.com/plugin/custom-player-models/versions
2) Download "player_models" folder
3) Move the "player_models" folder to the main folder (.minecraft\)
4) Enter the world and press G (default, "Gestures Menu") to open the menu, click "Models"
5) Choose one of the 2 models provided
6) Enjoy
